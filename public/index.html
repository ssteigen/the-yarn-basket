About

The Yarn Basket is your one-stop shop for knitting supplies.

Do you want to learn to knit? We regularly run classes!

Nestled in the heart of the Cumberland Valley lies a wonderful haven for knitters, and needlework enthusiasts. The Yarn Basket is Central Pennsylvania's Home for knitting and needlework. With our large selection of yarns, you can very easily make this your only stop for your needlework supplies.

The Yarn Basket is owned and run by Susan Yeager. Feel free to stop by and say "hi!"

Classes

Looking to improve your skills and what to have a great time doing so? From beginners to experts, we have a class for you.

Our goal is to teach you how to knit and purl, follow a pattern, and fix mistakes.

Choose a project and our instructors will help you complete it.

Class materials must be purchased from The Yarn Basket.

Additional Help

If you purchased your yarn from our store, we will always do our best to answer any questions you have.

Please call to see when the best time is to meet with one of our instructors.

Non-students can contact us to schedule an appointment for $8 per 15 minute slot.

These sessions must be schedule during off-peak hours. No walk ins, please.
